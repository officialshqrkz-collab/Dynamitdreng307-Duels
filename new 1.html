Duels

Duels is a first-person shooter (FPS) on Roblox created by Dynamitdreng307, a Danish Rivals YouTuber known for his competitive skill using luau and blender. Development began around April 2025, with the game taking heavy inspiration from Roblox Rivals and Classic Duels simple and fast-paced mechanics. Although still unfinished, Duels focuses on precision duels and skill-based combat, aiming to deliver short, intense matches. With strong community backing, and It being set to release sometime in March of 2026. it is already seen as a potential future cornerstone of Roblox FPS gaming.
